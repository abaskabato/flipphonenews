📱 Flip phone picks

Honest recommendations from someone who actually uses these things.

Cat S22 Flip ~$80

Rugged, runs Android. You get Maps, Signal, and a music player — but the screen is tiny enough that you won’t scroll. The gateway flip.
Nokia 2660 Flip ~$70

4G, FM radio, week-long battery. No apps, no browser, no excuses. The purest dumb phone you can buy new today.
Sunbeam F1 ~$290

The premium dumb phone. No browser, no app store. Curated dashboard with weather, calendar, and notes. Designed by people who get it.
Mive Style Folder ~$200

Korean import that actually looks good. Great camera, satisfying click, runs Android underneath. The flip for people who still care about aesthetics.
Punkt. MP02 ~$350

Privacy-first, Swiss-designed. Runs Signal and WhatsApp on a secure OS. No distractions, no tracking. The endgame dumb phone.
